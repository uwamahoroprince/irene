You too can help children in Malawi with our various support options

With your donation you give girls and boys in one of the poorest countries in the world a chance for a self-determined life through education.

On this page you will find an overview of how you can get involved with a financial donation: Online via our form, as a (free) donation when shopping or in the form of an occasional or large donation – every euro helps! Project-related donations are also possible – you will find them here under the heading “Projects” after selecting “Project donations”.

Are you interested in a long-term commitment? Here you will find information about our membership or project sponsorship.

Thank you very much for your commitment!

AmazonSmile: Shop online and do good

Do you use the Amazon portal for shopping? Then, on your next visit, designate the Christian Liebig Foundation as a beneficiary organization and make a donation without having to pay more yourself.

How it works: With every qualified purchase via AmazonSmile, Amazon passes on 0.5 percent of the purchase price to us. It is important that you enter via the URL smile.amazon.com (the web shop is identical) – it is best to set this link as a favourite.
